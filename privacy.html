Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. What We Collect

CC HUB collects only what is necessary to provide the service:

  • OAuth access tokens from connected platforms (TikTok, Instagram, YouTube) — stored locally on your device
  • Content you create or upload within the dashboard (titles, captions, scheduled times)
  • Basic account identifiers from connected platforms (e.g. username, user ID) to display in the UI
  • Analytics data fetched from your connected accounts (for display purposes only)

2. How We Use Your Data

We use collected information solely to:

  • Authenticate with connected platforms on your behalf
  • Upload and schedule content to platforms you have authorised
  • Display your analytics and content library
  • Save your planner tasks, notes, and content calendar locally

3. Data Storage

CC HUB is primarily a local application. Your data — including platform tokens, content, and settings — is stored in your browser's local storage on your device. We do not operate a centralised database or store your personal data on remote servers.

Platform access tokens are stored locally and are never transmitted to any third party other than the respective platform's official API endpoints.

4. Third-Party Platforms

When you connect TikTok, Instagram, or YouTube, you are granting CC HUB permission to act on your behalf through their official APIs. Each platform's own privacy policy governs how they handle your data:

  • TikTok Privacy Policy
  • Instagram / Meta Privacy Policy
  • Google / YouTube Privacy Policy

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third parties. The only data transmissions that occur are between CC HUB and the official API endpoints of platforms you have explicitly connected.

6. Data Deletion

You can disconnect any platform at any time from Settings, which removes the stored access tokens. To fully delete all local data, clear your browser's local storage for this application.

7. Cookies

CC HUB uses a single session cookie for authentication when accessed remotely. No tracking or advertising cookies are used.

8. Children's Privacy

CC HUB is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Contact

For any privacy-related questions or requests, contact us at cc@changingcurrents.co.uk
